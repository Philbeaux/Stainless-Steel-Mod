Normally found guarding the Grandmaster's quarters or other valuable things or places in service to the Order, these sword-wielding elite warriors make excellent fighters.
To keep out inquisitive eyes of the Temple, Grandmaster Guillaume de Sonnac deployed a Guard at its gates. The Templar Guard consisted of the Order's veteran Warriors, men which had proven their worth in battle. It is most rarely that these soldiers of the Temple are being called to fight among their brother Knights. But now, the Crescent united its powers against those of the Cross and the Templar Guard marches again.
