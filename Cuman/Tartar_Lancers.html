Tartar Lancers ride into battle dressed in lamellar armour and with a sharpened sword in hand.
Having lost their homelands to the Mongol Horde, these Volga Tartars have made their way to Lithuania, where they now look to establish a new society. When called on to defend their lands, they ride into battle dressed in lamellar armour and with a sharpened sword in hand, determined to succeed at the task they have once failed before.
