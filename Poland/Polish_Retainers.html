Wearing brigandine armour, and armed with a lance and sword, this company is capable of devastating charges.
These are the poorer and lower status cavalrymen that accompany the nobility to war. Unable to afford plate armour, these companies wear brigandine armour, padded cloth armour lined with small metal plates, and are armed with a lance and sword. Trained in heavy cavalry tactics this company is capable of devastating charges.
