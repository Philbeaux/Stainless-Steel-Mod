Spear Militia are commoners and peasants levied into local militias to defend settlements and bolster armies, armed with long spears.
Common throughout Europe, Spear Militia are commoners and peasants who have been levied into local militias to defend towns, roads and bolster armies. Given some training and equipped with a long spear and shield, these companies are useful in support roles for heavier and better companies.


  • Town Watch

  • Spear Militia are recruited with a Town Watch in a City or Town. (Note: With buildings from a City Watch up, Spear Militia are replaced by Pavise Spear Militia after the New Era event in 1300.)


  • Mustering Hall
  • Garrison Quarters

  • In Castles, Spear Militia are recruited with a Garrison Quarters. (Gain +1 XP)
