These noble Alani form an excellent cavalery unit that combines a spear charge with high manueverability. They are armored in light armor, shield and helmet. With deft handling of their spears, these horsemen make an unstopable chase unit.
Aldari (Alani) are recruited in the Steppes and the regions of Anatolia and Armenia.
