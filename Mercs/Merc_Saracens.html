Defense Skill: 9

Hit Points: 1

Recruitment Cost:Varies (420-460)

Upkeep: 420

These wild Eastern mercenaries are formidable soldiers ready to fight for whoever can afford them.
These wild and fierce mercenaries are well armoured and equipped with a scimitar. They ask a high price for their services and like most mercenaries are not too fussy about their employers. Once they charge into melee, most lords will soon forget how much they paid for them.

Mercenary Saracens are available after 1240.
