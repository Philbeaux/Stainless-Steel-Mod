A ship already in use for a thousand years. The Dhow is a trading vessel that can carry soldiers.
In use for over a thousand years the Dhow is a traditional Arab sailing vessel with one or more triangular 'Lateen' sails. These ships are used for trading but can be upgraded to carry warriors armed with bow and scimitar for assaulting enemy ships.


  • Port

  • Dhows are recruited in Islamic Cities or Castles with a Port
