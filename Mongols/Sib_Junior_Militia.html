Sibiryaki Junior Militia
The major craft in Siberia is hunting, and this is why the people of this land are master hunters, killing squirrels with a shaft through the eye in order to save the fur. In times of war, these men take their bows and their skill on to the field of battle. With no armor, they rely on speed and distance to protect them.


  • Town Watch
  • Town Guard

  • Sibiryaki Junior Militia are recruited in cities with a Town Guard.
