The Ghulams are soldiers largely drawn from captured slaves. Trained and kept at their "master's" expense, Ghulams are given their freedom once they reach a certain standard and are then kept on as their "master's" personal guard. These soldiers are well trained heavy cavalry, protected by heavy mail and equipped with a lance and a mace.
