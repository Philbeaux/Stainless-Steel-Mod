Encased in mail and equipped with spears and maces these soldiers are effective fighters.
Wealthy Khwarezmian cities have many men capable of equipping themselves as heavy cavalry, and turning out as militia in time of need. Well equipped, they might be mistaken for professional forces, but while effective against other militias, no part-time soldier can match the skill of men trained from birth.
