Persian Archers shower the enemy with arrows, weakening and breaking formations before the infantry move in.
Equipped with a composite bow and a conical shield, these excellent warriors are militia from Persia, who have left their homelands and now fight in the armies of Khwarezm. They are expert archers equipped with powerful bows. They usually wear some light armour underneath their robes.
