Both the raider and the horse are protected by excellent armour, giving these horse archers the best protection against enemy attacks. Armoured Horsearchers companies are expert bowmen that use evasive tactics to their fullest, much to the frustration of their enemies. Their excellent heavy armour is financed directly by the treasury of the Shah. When the need to engage arises, they make good use of their iron swords.
