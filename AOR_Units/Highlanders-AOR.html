Fierce and wild warriors from the Scottish Highlands. These hardy and reckless warriors, accustomed to in-fighting and raiding for loot and slaves, fight with little to no armour, a shield and are equipped with an axe. Notoriously ill disciplined these warriors can pack a punch but are no match for disciplined and armoured companies in formation.


Highlanders are recruited in Castles in the Scottish Homeland in the regions of the Scottish Highlands, Scottish Lowlands and Grampian.

  • Mustering Hall
  • Garrison Quarters

  • In Castles, Highlanders are recruited in Garrison Quarters.
