Well skilled soldiers armed with a crossbow and sword and armoured in plate.
These men are highly skilled in the use of many arms including the crossbow and sword. Primarily utilised as missile soldiers using their crossbows, they are so well armoured with plate and skilled with a sword that they can also be used as shock soldiers when called upon.
