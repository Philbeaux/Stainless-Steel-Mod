Ritualistic worshipers of Perkunas, these men enter the fray with a war scythe in hand.
Perkunas, as the Pagan God of thunder and rain, draws especially strong worship from farmers and woodsmen. When the call to arms is made, Perkunas' Followers don their robes of worship to perform a sacrificial ritual, which they believe will grant them divine strength, before joining the field of battle with war scythe in hand.


  • Alter of Perkunas
  • Sanctuary of Perkunas

  • Followers of Perkunas are available in cities that have constructed a Sanctuary of Perkunas
