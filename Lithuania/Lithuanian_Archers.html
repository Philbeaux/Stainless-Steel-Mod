Skilled archery company lightly armoured but equipped with a large shield. Able to lay stakes at start of battle.
Lithuanian soldiers are often recruited by Polish nobles to serve in their armies. Lithuanian archers are especially recruited for their fine skill on the battlefield. Like most missile soldiers they are lightly armoured, although they have a large shield. Lithuanian archers are able to lay fiendish stakes in front of them at the start of a battle.
