Mounted on a camel this company fires a powerful musket called a "jezail'.
These gunners use a type of heavy matchlock musket, which fires a powerful shot. Combine this firepower with a camel and the end result is a powerful and very mobile missile company that is ideal for desert warfare. As an added bonus the camels scare horses.

  • Caravan Stop

  • In Castles, Camel Gunners are recruited with a Caravansary.
