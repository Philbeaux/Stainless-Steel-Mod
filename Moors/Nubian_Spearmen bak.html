    Nubian Spearmen are African slaves from the lands south of the Sahara desert, trained and formed into standing military companies. They are brave and resilient, but lightly armed with short spears, shields, and lacking armour. While useful at defending flanks and dealing with light cavalry, these soldiers won't hold up in a prolonged melee.
